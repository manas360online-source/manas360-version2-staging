## 🎯 How to Use

### Option 1: Quick Assessment (1 minute)

**Path**: Dashboard → Assessments → Quick Mental Check tab

1. Rate 6 dimensions on 0-4 scale:
   - Mood
   - Sleep quality
   - Stress load
   - Energy level
   - Focus ability
   - Social connection

2. Click "Submit Quick Check"
3. View results with severity level + recommendations

---

### Option 2: Daily Assessment (2 minutes) **NEW!**

**Path**: Dashboard → Assessments → Daily Assessment tab

1. Answer 5 quick questions (0-10 scale):
   - **Mood today** (How are you feeling?)
   - **Sleep quality** (How well did you sleep?)
   - **Energy level** (How energized are you?)
   - **Anxiety level** (0=none, 10=severe)
   - **Overall wellbeing** (0=poor, 10=excellent)

2. See real-time feedback:
   - ✓ Good (7+) - Green indicator
   - ◐ Moderate (4-6) - Orange indicator
   - ✗ Low (<4) - Red indicator

3. Click "Submit Daily Check"
4. Results auto-save with timestamp

**💡 Pro Tip**: Complete one daily assessment each morning to establish a tracking habit!

---

### Option 3: Clinical Assessment (5 minutes)

**Path**: Dashboard → Assessments → Clinical Assessments tab

1. Choose one:
   - **PHQ-9** - Depression screening
   - **GAD-7** - Anxiety screening
   - **PSS-10** - Stress evaluation
   - **ISI** - Sleep quality assessment

2. Move slider to your score
3. Click "Save Clinical Assessment"
4. Get personalized recommendations based on results

## 📊 View Your Analytics Report

**Path**: Dashboard → Progress → Assessment Analytics **OR** Dashboard → Assessments → [See History Below]

### In Assessment Page - Assessment History Section
- See your last 20 assessments
- Shows: Date, Type, Score, Severity Level
- Color-coded: 🔴 Severe, 🟠 Moderate, 🟢 Mild
- Click "Refresh History" to reload

### Full Analytics Dashboard
<br>**Path**: `/patient/assessment-reports`

#### Step 1: Filter Your Data
- **Assessment Type**: All / Quick Check / Clinical / Daily
- **Time Range**: Last Week / Last Month / Last Year

#### Step 2: View Key Metrics
Cards showing:
- **Total Assessments** - How many you completed
- **Average Score** - Your typical score
- **Trend** - Are you improving? 📈 📉 ➡️
- **Severity Breakdown** - Count of Severe/Moderate/Mild

#### Step 3: Review Charts
- **Score Trend** - 14-day bar chart showing your progress
- **Severity Distribution** - Pie chart of assessment levels
- **Recent Assessments Table** - Detailed list of last 10

#### Step 4: Read AI Recommendations

The system will show:

✅ **If Improving**:
> "Great progress! Your overall wellbeing is trending upward. Continue with your current approach."

⚠️ **If Declining**:
> "Attention needed. Your scores are declining. Consider scheduling a session with your therapist."

➡️ **If Stable**:
> "Status quo: Your wellbeing is stable. Consistency is key to improvement."

🚨 **If High Severity**:
> "High severity noted. Please reach out to your care team."

**Next Steps** (always shown):
- Continue daily or weekly assessments to track patterns
- Share this report with your therapist
- Practice recommended exercises from your therapy plan

## 📈 Understanding Your Trends

### What Makes a "Good" Trend?

**Improving** (📈):
- Your recent scores are higher than older scores
- Green light for continuing current activities
- Consider sharing progress with your therapist

**Declining** (📉):
- Your recent scores are lower than older scores
- Red flag to pay attention to wellbeing
- Good time to reach out to care team

**Stable** (➡️):
- Your scores stay consistent
- Neutral signal - room for improvement but not worse
- Consider adding new wellness practices

## 🎯 Best Practices

### Daily Assessment Habit
- ⏰ Complete at same time each day (e.g., morning with coffee)
- 📱 Takes only 2-3 minutes
- 📊 After 2 weeks, patterns emerge
- 🎯 Set weekly goal: 5/7 days

### Combined Approach
- **Daily**: Use daily assessment for continuous monitoring

1. Complete your assessments
2. Go to Assessment Analytics
3. Share screenshot or full report with therapist

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Your assessments are stored securely in MANAS360. They appear in your assessment history and analytics dashboard.

**Q: Can I delete past assessments?**
A: Currently, assessments are archived but not deletable. Contact support if you need data removed.

**Q: Should I do multiple assessment types?**
A: Yes! Different assessments measure different things:
- Daily Check: Mood & wellbeing snapshot
- Quick Check: Broader mental health picture
- Clinical: Specific clinical measurements (PHQ-9 depression, etc)

**Q: What's the difference between Daily and Quick Check?**
- **Daily**: 5 questions, focus on NOW (today's mood/energy)
- **Quick**: 6 questions, broader check (stress, focus, connection)

**Q: Can my therapist see these?**
A: Your therapist can see assessments you share. You control what they see via your dashboard and reports.

**Q: How often should I do assessments?**
A: Daily (daily check), weekly (quick check), and monthly (clinical) is ideal for good tracking.
