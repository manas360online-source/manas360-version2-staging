3 Core Parameters (Patient → Provider)

🎯
Expertise Match
40
Clinical competency alignment through specialization overlap and professional credentials
• Specialization: 0-30 pts (must match ≥1 concern)
• Certification: 0-10 pts (count + level bonus)
// Example
concerns: Anxiety, Depression
provider: Anxiety, Depression, Trauma + CBT (Advanced)
→ spec:22 + cert:8 = 30/40
💬
Communication & Logistics
35
Practical feasibility of engagement through language, schedule, and delivery mode alignment
• Language: 0-20 pts (must match ≥1 language)
• Availability: 0-10 pts (time slot overlap)
• Mode: 0-5 pts (video/phone/in-person)
// Example
languages: English, Hindi → 20 pts
time: Evening → 8 pts
mode: Video → 5 pts
→ 33/35
⭐
Provider Quality
25
Track record and proven performance through platform metrics and patient history
• Platform Health: 0-15 pts (rating + retention + no-show)
• Continuity: 0-10 pts (prior sessions with patient)
// Example
rating: 4.8, retention: 68%, no-show: 2% → 13 pts
prior sessions: 6 → 10 pts
→ 23/25
REMOVED Velocity (SQL filter: weekly_sessions < 20)
REMOVED Service Willingness (SQL filter: accepts_service_type = true)
